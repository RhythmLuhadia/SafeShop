# AI/ML Engineer Roadmap - The Core Pillars & Timeline

To be hired and succeed as an AI/ML Engineer, you need a specific blend of three worlds: the statistical knowledge of a Data Scientist, the coding skills of a Software Engineer, and the deployment skills of a Cloud/DevOps Engineer. 

---

### STRATEGY: Balancing with DSA (The 3-Month Placement Crunch)
Since you only have **3 months** until placement season, you MUST prioritize strategically. 
*   **The Reality of Placements:** You can have the most incredible AI projects on your resume, but if you cannot pass the initial Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA) Online Assessment (OA), the interviewer will never look at your resume.
*   **Time Split:** Commit **40% of your time to DSA** (LeetCode / NeetCode 150) and **60% to ML Engineering**.
*   **The Order:** Yes, you should learn the concepts roughly in the order below, as they build on top of each other (You can't deploy a Docker container if you don't know FastAPI first).

Here is your combined 3-Month Timeline:

#### Month 1: Foundations & Backend (Weeks 1 - 4)
*   **DSA Focus:** Arrays, Hashing, Two Pointers, Strings, Linked Lists.
*   **ML Focus (Python OOP & APIs):**
    *   Transition your existing model code into clean OOP classes.
    *   Learn FastAPI to wrap your models into working endpoints.
*   **Deliverable for Resume:** Convert an existing ML Notebook (like your SafeShop project) into a modular FastAPI application that accepts requests via Postman.

#### Month 2: Containerization & Modern AI (Weeks 5 - 8)
*   **DSA Focus:** Trees, Binary Search, Sliding Window, Backtracking.
*   **ML Focus (Docker & HuggingFace):**
    *   Learn Docker to containerize your FastAPI apps.
    *   Learn the Hugging Face ecosystem (Transformers, NLP pipelines).
*   **Deliverable for Resume:** Containerize the API you built in Month 1 using Docker. Build a small RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) application.

#### Month 3: Cloud Deployment & MLOps (Weeks 9 - 12)
*   **DSA Focus:** Graphs, Dynamic Programming, and Daily Mock Interviews.
*   **ML Focus (Cloud & Tracking):**
    *   Learn MLflow to track model metrics.
    *   Learn AWS/Cloud basics to deploy your Docker containers to the internet.
*   **Deliverable for Resume:** Deploy your containerized API to the cloud (e.g., Render, AWS EC2) so it has a live URL you can put on your resume.
